// FAQ
Do I need to install anything?

No. PhishGuard runs entirely in your browser. Paste an email, get results. No downloads, no plugins, no configuration.

How accurate is the detection?

PhishGuard uses a multi-engine approach combining heuristic analysis, URL pattern matching, header forensics, and social engineering detection. Our detection accuracy is competitive with commercial solutions for common phishing tactics.

Is my data secure?

Demo scans are ephemeral — no data is stored server-side. For registered users, analysis data is encrypted at rest with AES-256. We never sell or share your data.

Can I scan my Microsoft 365 or Gmail inbox?

Yes. PhishGuard supports IMAP-based inbox scanning for Gmail (app passwords) and Microsoft 365. OAuth-based Microsoft Graph integration is coming soon for a zero-config experience.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel anytime from your billing dashboard. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
