### Maker Comment
```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

We built PhishGuard AI because we realized most phishing tools are either:
1. Enterprise-only (Proofpoint, Abnormal — $50k+/year)
2. Too technical (requires configuring SPF/DKIM/DMARC, SIEM integration)
3. Or just don't exist for the average small business

PhishGuard is different:
→ Paste any email → get a risk score in under 3 seconds
→ No installation, no configuration, no credit card
→ Free tier: 10 scans/month forever
→ Open source core (MIT)
→ PDF reports, OSINT enrichment, social engineering analysis

We're targeting the 99% of businesses that don't have a security team but still get phished every day.

Would love your feedback! 🛡
```
